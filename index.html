Delete Your US2 Account

Last updated: June 2026

If you would like to permanently delete your US2 account and associated data, you can request deletion by contacting the developer using the email address below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

us2.support1@gmail.com

Please use the subject:

Delete My US2 Account

Include the email address that you use to sign in to the US2 application.

What Will Be Deleted

After your request has been verified, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user account
  • Your profile information
  • Your profile image
  • Your personal calendar events
  • Your personal notes
  • Your shared notes and shared calendar events associated with your account
  • Your partner connection information
  • Your notification preferences

Retention Period

Your account deletion request will normally be completed within 30 days.

Some information may be retained only if required by applicable law.
